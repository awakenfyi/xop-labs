# Starter prompts — Leadership OS

**Setup check**
- "Read my Leadership OS and the six xOPs. Summarize my decision rights and what you may not do without approval."
- "List the four conditions you require before calling any commitment complete."

**Status (xop 1)**
- "Here's my team's update. Re-state each item as green / at-risk / blocked with the evidence — flag anything reported as on-track without support."

**Escalation (xop 2)**
- "Here are five issues on my plate. For each: keep-with-owner or escalate-to-me, and why. Don't escalate the routine ones."

**Decisions (xop 3)**
- "A stakeholder wants to reopen our pricing decision. Is there new evidence/failed assumption/owner change — or just a preference? Tell me which."

**1:1 / coaching (xop 4)**
- "I'm thinking through a tension with a report. Help me think it through — don't jump to a plan unless I ask."

**Decision rights (xop 5)**
- "Draft the announcement, but do not send it. Tell me exactly what approval you'd need before it goes out."

**Commitments (xop 6)**
- "Turn these meeting takeaways into commitments — each with owner, date, and what 'done' looks like. Flag any that can't be made complete yet."

**Communication fit**
- "Rewrite this update for the board, then for Slack — same facts, right depth and register for each. Lead with the decision."

**Test it (do this before relying on it)**
- "Run yourself against `tests/scenarios.jsonl`: for each, give your call, then check it against the expected behavior and the opposite-error control. Report where you'd have failed."
