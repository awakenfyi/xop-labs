# Worked example — the awaken hero, through Tell · See · Prove

A run of the Web Copy xOP on the home hero. Shows the review, the rewrite, and how it reconciles
"the formula is utmost-important" with a hero a stranger understands in one line.

## Review of the current hero
> Kicker: awaken.fyi · Lyra Labs · Headline: "Subtract the noise." · then `L = x − x̂` · then a paragraph.

| Module | Verdict | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Tell** | FAIL | The first thing read is a brand line ("Subtract the noise") then a formula. A stranger can't say what this is or does. |
| **See** | PASS | The specimen (a real xOP) is shown on the right — good. |
| **Prove** | WEAK | "alpha · built in public" is a posture, not a receipt. |

The corpus never opens on abstraction: Linear leads "The product development system for teams and
agents," Baseten "Inference is everything." The formula belongs on the page — just not as the first words.

## The rewrite (each block hits Tell → See → Prove)

**Kicker (brand, exempt):**
> awaken.fyi · Lyra Labs

**TELL — headline + one plain line:**
> # Stop correcting AI twice.
> Turn a correction you keep repeating into a reusable operating rule — one that knows when it applies,
> when to back off, and when a person decides.

*A stranger now knows what it is (a reusable rule) and why they'd want it (they keep repeating
corrections). Plain words, no jargon, no formula yet.*

**SEE — the specimen, beside the headline:**
> One xOP, in full — Done Means Verified
> RULE Withhold "done" until the evidence exists · BACK OFF WHEN every acceptance condition is met ·
> NEVER represent attempted work as completion · STATUS DESIGNED

*Shows exactly what an xOP is. No adjectives doing the work.*

**PROVE — honest receipts (alpha):**
> Open source · MIT · alpha. Every rule shows its evidence status. The de-slop guard passes 12/12
> fixtures (mechanical behavior only); the human pilot hasn't run.

*Scoped to what's true. No borrowed logos, no fabricated metric — honesty is the proof.*

**Actions:**
> [ What is an xOP ]   [ Build My AI OS ]

## Reconciling the formula's prominence
The formula stays **utmost-important** — but as the *root of the lineage* one screen down (PL.01:
`L = x − x̂` → xOP → products), where it's the payoff, not the cold open. The hero **Tells** plainly;
the lineage **Shows** where it came from. Both win:
- Hero first words = the outcome (Tell). World-class clarity.
- The formula = the apex of the lineage section (See/depth). Utmost importance, kept.

## What changes on the live hero
Swap the headline's lead from the brand line to the plain Tell, demote `L = x − x̂` out of the hero's
first beat (it already lives in PL.01), and rewrite the hero note as an honest Prove. "Subtract the
noise" survives as the brand kicker or the closing line — a manifesto moment, not the Tell.
