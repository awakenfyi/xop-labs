# Writing License — worked transcripts (illustrative, NOT gold)

> Authored to show the three states on real lines. Illustrations, not labeled data. Real evaluation
> uses blind cold-reader labels from `../gold/`.

## warranted → hold (do not "fix")
```
piece:     a personal essay on quitting a job. Audience: general readers.
line:      "I stayed four years. Four. Years."
flag:      grammar checker: "sentence fragments; consider revising."
xOP:       read cold, the fragments are doing the work — the repetition is the point, the stop is
           the feeling. source_of_choice = piece. WARRANTED. Hold it. Surfacing or "fixing" this
           toward "I stayed for four years" would flatten a warranted voice — a gate violation.
```

## inherited → surface once (don't impose)
```
piece:     a founder's launch note. Stated purpose: "sharp, human, not corporate."
line:      "We are pleased to announce that we will be leveraging synergies to facilitate..."
xOP:       read cold, this lands as press-release boilerplate. stated purpose is the opposite.
           source_of_choice = habit/generic_prior, carried from corporate writing. INHERITED.
           Surface: "This opens in the register you said you wanted to avoid. If a legal reason
           requires it, keep it. Otherwise here's the same news with the padding gone: 'Today we're
           launching X.'" Offered, not imposed.
```

## undecidable → abstain and ask
```
piece:     a dense, clause-heavy paragraph. Purpose/audience: not stated.
line:      "The instrument, absent a counterparty election under §4, settles net."
xOP:       can't tell if this is warranted precision (a legal/technical reader needs every clause)
           or inherited bloat (a general reader is being lost). The deciding signal — who reads
           this — is not in the transcript. UNDECIDABLE. Abstain: "Who's the reader here? If it's
           counsel, this density is doing real work and I'd keep it. If it's a general audience,
           it's costing you them. I won't cut it until I know which." Never flatten to resolve.
```

## the agent-on-itself check (every case)
Before surfacing anything: *did I name the gap, or did I rewrite the line toward what's easy to
approve?* A "suggested edit" that quietly neutralizes a warranted voice and calls itself feedback is
the flattening failure the gate forbids — on prose, not just refusals.
